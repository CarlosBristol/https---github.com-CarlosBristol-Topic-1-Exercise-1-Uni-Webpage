Computing at the University of Northampton

We offer a full range of flexible, part-time and full-time, undergraduate and postgraduate programmes to help you develop key computing skills. Our courses offer an excellent opportunity to gain employment or to develop skills essential for career enhancement and progression to ensure that you can excel in whichever career you choose to enter. We welcome all applicants to our courses, including international applications and applications from students with a range of non-traditional educational or professional qualifications.
